# Project Ecoquest

![Ecoquest Logo](link-to-your-logo.png)

Welcome to Project Ecoquest! Our mission is to empower students and schools to make a positive impact on the environment through sustainable practices.

## About

Ecoquest is an innovative platform that engages students, teachers, and school management in the journey toward greater energy efficiency and sustainability. Our gamified approach makes learning fun and interactive, encouraging everyone to participate actively.

## Features

- **Gamification:** Engage in friendly competitions and earn rewards.
- **Real-time Insights:** Monitor energy usage in real-time.
- **Learning Hub:** Explore a comprehensive sustainability resource center.
